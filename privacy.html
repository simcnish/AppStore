Privacy Policy for Workout Timer

Effective Date: November 23, 2025
Last Updated: November 23, 2025

Introduction

McNish Development ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Workout Timer mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Workout Timer is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your workout data should remain private and under your control.

Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • ❌ We do NOT collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ We do NOT collect location data
  • ❌ We do NOT collect workout history or performance data
  • ❌ We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • ❌ We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT require account creation or login
  • ❌ We do NOT collect device identifiers for tracking purposes

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device only:

  1. Timer Settings and Preferences
    • Your configured timer intervals (work time, rest time, etc.)
    • Number of sets and repetitions
    • Audio preference settings
    • Last used timer configurations

This information is stored using AsyncStorage (React Native's local storage system) and never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

How We Use Information

The locally stored settings are used solely to:

  • Remember your timer configurations between app sessions
  • Provide a seamless user experience
  • Eliminate the need to reconfigure timers each time you use the App

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • Data remains on your device until you delete the App
  • You can clear all stored data by deleting and reinstalling the App

Audio Permissions

The App uses audio to provide:

  • Countdown beeps
  • Transition sounds between intervals
  • Warning beeps before interval changes

Audio Permission Usage:

  • The App plays audio through your device speakers
  • The App does NOT record audio
  • The App does NOT access your microphone
  • The App does NOT access your music library
  • Audio continues to play when the App is in the background (for workout continuity)

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's use of the App.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) do not apply. However:

  • You can delete all locally stored settings by uninstalling the App
  • You can reset settings by clearing the App's data through iOS Settings
  • You have complete control over your device's local storage
